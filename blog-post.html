Can you be autor?

  • By : By: Lionel Messi
  • 29 Nov, 2021
  • 35

There are plenty of authors who write for love of the craft. Most of us, though, desire for our carefully crafted words to be read by the masses as we rake in millions. When it comes to literary validation, hitting the best-seller lists is one of the most universally recognized achievements.

Classic – a book which people praise and don’t read.

But the reality is that landing on high-powered rankings takes more than just talent and a killer plot. Indeed, becoming a best-selling author is just as much about marketing and promotion as the actual writing. That’s a scary notion for us introverted folks who would much rather hide behind our bylines than scream from the mountaintops for readers to “buy my book — now!” So with that in mind, we’ve culled a list of tips to help ensure that your next book reaches more than the members of your creative writing group. Follow these six steps, and you’ll be a certified best-seller in no time.
